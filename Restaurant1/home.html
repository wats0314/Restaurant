You've come to the right place! Slammin' Sam's 60's Surside Shack has been dishing up California's best handcrafted burgers since 1994. In 1993, Mitch started off on the beach with a grill serving up his family's burger recipe to surfers and passers by. As word got out, Mitch could no longer keep up with demand. He quit his full time job as a web developer, and opened his own restaurant right here in Ventura, CA! Since then, we have become a world renowned dive, and we have been featured on Food Network's "Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives" featuring Guy Fieri. Come on on down to Sammy's and enjoy a fantastic burger and a 60s surfer vibe that is totally "far out!"
