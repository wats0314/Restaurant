You've come to the right place! Corona Mitch's Burger Shack has been dishing up California's best handcrafted burgers since 1994. In 1993, Mitch started off on the beach with a grill serving up his family's burger recipe to surfers and passers by. As word got out, Mitch could no longer keep up with demand. He quit his full time job as an HTML developer, and opened his own restaurant right here in Corona, CA! Since then, we have become a world renowned dive, and we have been featured on Food Network's "Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives" featuring Guy Fieri. Come on on down to Corona Mitch's and enjoy our famous "Corona Cali Burger" a shake, and a side of fries. We promise, it lives up to the hype!
