"You've come to the right place!"


In 1994, Sam started off on the beach with a grill serving up his family's burger recipe to surfers and passers by. As word got out, Sam could no longer keep up with demand. He quit his full time job, and opened his own restaurant right here in Ventura, CA! Since then, we have become a world renowned dive, and we have been featured on Food Network's "Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives" featuring Guy Fieri. Come on on down to Sammy's and enjoy a fantastic burger and a 60s surfer vibe that is totally "far out!"
